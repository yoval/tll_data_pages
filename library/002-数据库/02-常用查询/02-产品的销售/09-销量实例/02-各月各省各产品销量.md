### 各产品各省销量

```sql
SELECT 
    LEFT(business_date, 6) AS 月份,
    prov_name AS 省份,
    item_name AS 产品名称,
    SUM(dp_item_count) AS 销量,
    SUM(dp_total_amount) AS 流水
FROM 
    ads_dbs_trade_food_di
WHERE 
    business_date BETWEEN 20240801 AND 20240930
GROUP BY 
    月份, 省份, 产品名称
HAVING 
    SUM(dp_item_count) > 0;
```
